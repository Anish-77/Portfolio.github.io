Sales Dashboard-PowerBI

A Power BI dashboard is a single page, often called a canvas, that uses visualizations to tell a story. Because it's limited to one page, a well-designed dashboard contains only the most important elements of that story.

The Global Superstores Power BI Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into the performance and trends of the company's sales, inventory, and customer data across various regions and product categories. It offers interactive visualizations and analytics, allowing stakeholders to quickly identify patterns, analyze profitability, track key performance indicators (KPIs), and make informed business decisions. The dashboard integrates data from multiple sources, enabling users to drill down into specific metrics and gain actionable insights for optimizing operations and driving growth.
